    • SkyeWalker Law

    SkyeWalker Law, a UAE Law Firm with affiliate offices in London and New York, provides legal, consultancy and deal origination services to its clients across the Globe, and specializes particularly in complex cross-border and corporate legal matters. It also finds funding and assists clients in UAE business growth generally, including by distribution, franchising and other commercial arrangements, often using a range of its partners.

    SkyeWalker Law has extensive global expertise in advising UHNW individuals and large corporates, through to fast growth start-ups, on corporate and commercial law, M&A, finance, joint ventures, distribution deals, licensing and franchising agreements, corporate structuring, cross border transactions, business transactions, strategic advice on impending disputes and working with local counsel. The firm and its partners adhere to the principles of English Law and are specialists in Dubai and Hong Kong law.
